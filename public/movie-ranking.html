Michael's New Movie Recommendations

My top movies with 2022 and 2023 release dates.

Bullet Train - Action Comedy (2022)

A very fast paced movie loaded with humor

The Man from Toronto - Action Comedy (2022)

If you love Kevin heart, comedy, and spy movies this is the film for you!

The Lost City - Romantic Comedy/Action (2022)

Sandra Bullock and Channing Tatum star in a goofy action comedy with just enough romance to make it interesting.

Plane - Action (2023)

This fast paced action movies starring Gerard Butler is great for those wanting a feel good action movie.

Creed III - Action (2023)

The third installment in the Creed series does not disappoint. For those who love the thrill of a good boxing movie with a great storyline, this is for you!
